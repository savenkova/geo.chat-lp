Chats

Geo.chat store messages, photos, videos and other documents from your chats on our servers, so that you can access your data from any of your devices anytime.

Geo.chat uses phone numbers as unique identifiers.

Geo.chat requests your permission to have your location displayed on the map and uses information about your geolocation in order to help you to find a chats around you. You can hide your geolocation if you want so.
